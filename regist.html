Sign Up to Play an Event

Dungeons & Dragons:

To reserve a spot, simply select the night you wish to attend and go through our online check-out process. Requirements: This event is open to all experience levels. Cost: $10 per player.

Warhammer 40K:

Gamescape hosts an open and friendly community, plenty of table space, and a fabulous terrain selection all for free use. Free demo games featuring fully-painted armies are available for new or interested players to have a go, just give us a call and let us know you're coming prior to the event.

Experience: All skill-levels are welcome. Demos for newcomers will be available by appointment.

Cost: Free Tournament Play Experience: Players should have a legal army and enough prior experience to play their army unassisted. Cost: $20

Magic the Gathering:

Drafts: Improvise as you open packs on the spot! Build unique decks and battle with the cards you acquire. Requirements: Players should be familiar enough with the rules of the game to construct a deck and play against an opponent. Please preregister for the new Wizards event software prior to the event. Cost: $15 per player.
